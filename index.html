Labour Economics · Machine Learning · Streamlit App

The Hidden Work Gap

Ireland's gig economy hides a structural problem no official metric captures. I invented my own: the Hidden Work Gap — a composite signal combining underemployment rate, income volatility, and benefit exclusion. Built a Random Forest classifier (78% accuracy), a normalised SQL schema, 10+ EDA charts, and a live Streamlit app where you can score any worker profile in real time.

"The data shows what policy reports won't say out loud — Gen Z gig workers are falling through every safety net simultaneously."
